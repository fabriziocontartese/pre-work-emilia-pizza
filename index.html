Pizzas

0. Ingredients

We source only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients directly from local farmers and renowned Italian producers. From San Marzano tomatoes to fragrant basil and creamy buffalo mozzarella, every ingredient tells a story of tradition and flavor.

1. Dough

Our doughs (napolitan, roman, sicilian, new york, sourdough, etc.) are crafted following a meticulous 48-hour fermentation process. Using authentic '00' flour and pure mountain water, each dough ball is carefully hand-stretched to achieve that perfect lightness and irresistible chew.

2. Cooking

Each pizza is cooked with a different method, wood, and temperature based on the style. This brings out the unique, blistering, and a delicate flavours of each creation.

3. Pairings

Enhance your pizza experience with expertly paired wines, craft cocktails, and other concuctions. Each beverage is thoughtfully selected to complement and elevate the unique flavors of our pizzas.

Spaces

Yesterday

Step back in time to our historic 14th-century château, lovingly preserved with its original architecture. Once home to Emilia's family, the walls carry the charm of centuries, setting the scene for a timeless culinary journey.

Today

Emilia Pizza blends the elegance of historical charm with modern comforts. Our restaurant features intimate dining rooms, each uniquely designed to reflect Emilia's travels, creating an atmosphere that's both refined and welcoming.

Tomorrow

The future holds limitless possibilities with our adaptable event space. From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, every visit promises a unique experience, creatively tailored to make each occasion unforgettable.
